## Name
DriftLogic — Rocky River Rigs

## Subtitle (30 chars max)
Steelhead rig builder, Ohio

## Description

Answer six quick questions and DriftLogic builds your complete Rocky River rig — rod and line, leader, tippet, five flies or lures matched to conditions, the reasoning behind every choice, and a pro tip from local steelhead know-how.

Built for Northeast Ohio's Rocky River and tuned for Lake Erie tributary fishing:

• Four targets: Steelhead, Smallmouth Bass, Walleye, Channel Catfish
• Three methods: Fly, Spinning, Center-Pin
• Live river conditions — pulls the USGS Rocky River gauge in real time and fills in current, clarity, and water temp for you
• Hatch matching for fly anglers: eggs, BWO, caddis, midge, stonefly
• "Why This Rig" — where fish hold, how flow and temp change the bite, and how clarity drives presentation
• Five hand-picked technique videos for every species and method
• Works fully offline (live gauge optional)

Every recommendation is generated by a decision engine validated across all 4,860 possible condition combinations.

## Privacy nutrition label
- Data collected: **none**. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking.
- Network use: a single anonymous GET to waterservices.usgs.gov (public river gauge) and YouTube thumbnail images. Nothing is sent.
- Outbound links: YouTube (videos), Amazon and FishUSA (gear searches; Amazon links carry the rockyriver-20 Associates tag — disclosed in-app).

## App Review notes (paste into the Review Notes field)
The app is a self-contained recommendation tool; no login needed. The "Rocky River now" banner requires network to query the public USGS water-data API; if offline it hides itself. Shop buttons open Amazon/FishUSA search results in the browser; the Amazon link uses an affiliate tag and the app displays an Amazon Associates disclosure under the picks list.
